SideOut King & Queen of the court



Described as "a new era in beach volleyball", this new, exciting and ultra-fast paced tournament sees up to 5 teams play against each other on each single court, until one male and one female team are left standing and crowned King and Queen of the Court!

If you have ever played the classic "king of the court" or "queen of the court" in training, you will know how this works. In short, the objective is to get to the "king" or "queen" side of the court and stay there for as long as possible by siding out and accumulating points! After each rally, the losing team leaves the court, with the winning team moving to, or staying on, the "king" or "queen" side.

The action is fast and furious, so be prepared! Remember: this is not a mixed tournament and you sign up as a team not individually! See you on the sand!
