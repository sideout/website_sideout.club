THE SPORT

Beach volleyball is not only a fun sport that is played recreationally on beaches all over the world, but it is also a unique, dynamic and incredibly popular Olympic sport. Since the iconic London 2012 Olympic beach volleyball tournament at Horse Guards Parade, the sport has been rapidly growing here in the UK too and the future is bright for youngsters keen to get involved - whether they have ever played before or not!

OUR PROGRAMME

The SideOut Junior Beach Programme in Worthing offers boys and girls aged 5-12 the chance to train, play and compete.

The programme is managed and delivered by a highly experienced team of coaches. Our goal is to combine fun with top quality training and playing opportunities for boys and girls of all age groups and abilities in a friendly and safe environment where they can make new friends, improve their fitness, develop their skills and - should they eventually choose to - pursue opportunities to compete at local, national or even international level!

All activities take place at SideOut Beach in Worthing.

Check out our Events Calendar and click on the red "Juniors" button to find out what we have on offer throughout the season, including the SideOut Junior Series beach volleyball tournaments!

Junior Beach Volleyball Training, Worthing: Frequently Asked Questions


WHEN ARE THE SESSIONS?

Junior sessions (for ages 5-12) are every Saturday from 12:00 noon to 1:00pm..

WHAT'S THE JUNIORS' LEVEL?

We welcome kids of all levels - from complete beginners, to more experienced players who already train or compete regularly in either indoor or beach volleyball. Groups are split by age group and ability, so everyone will always be training and playing in an appropriate group.

WHO ARE THE COACHES?

We have assembled a team of extremely passionate and highly skilled coaches with diverse and extensive playing and coaching experience at all levels of the sport. All of our coaches are fully qualified and DBS checked and our Club is affiliated with Volleyball England, beach volleyball's national governing body.

WHAT DOES IT COST?

£4 per session if a full month is purchased in advance, or £5 per single session.

* All junior players must also have a valid Junior Club Membership, which costs £11 per person and is valid for the entire season.

HOW DO WE SIGN UP?

Go to our Registration page and then:

(a) Log in to your profile, or create one for free if you don't already have one.

(b) Select "Junior Membership" from the list and follow the instructions to register and pay online. The cost is just £11 for the season and that's it - you are officially a Junior Member of the Club!

(c) Next, select "Junior Training" from the same list and follow the instructions to register and pay.

HOW CAN WE PAY?

Membership must be paid for online by PayPal. You do not need a PayPal account, just a debit or credit card. Simply follow the link provided when registration is complete.

Training sessions must be paid for by bank transfer. We will provide you with payment details once you have signed up online.

If you would like to discuss flexible payment options, please feel free to email us using the contact details at the bottom of this page!

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

After you've signed up online, we will contact you directly by email to give you further information about your upcoming sessions.

STILL HAVE QUESTIONS?

We'd be happy to answer them! Simply get in touch!
