Club History

Our club history starts in 2011.


Summer 2011: SideOut is founded.

Summer 2012: Started using "Leyton Beach" for occasional weekend tournaments and training camps.

Summer 2013: Continued to use Leyton Beach on weekends and the occasional weekday.

Spring 2014: Leyton Beach becomes our permanent home and is renamed "SideOut Beach". We add an extra court and go up from 3 to 4 courts. We start hosting London Beach Volleyball Club's daily training sessions and providing coaches for LBVC's sessions.

Spring 2015: SideOut Beach Volleyball Club starts to offer its own training sessions and tournaments. The venue is open 7 days a week from 1 April to 30 September and occasional days in the autumn and winter months.

2016: The Club continues to grow, the clubhouse undergoes refurbishment and floodlights are added to the venue.

Spring 2017: We extend the sand-pit and add a 5th court, allowing us to host more training sessions and larger tournaments. The clubhouse is refurbished again too!

Spring & Summer 2018: Our last season at the original SideOut Beach comes to an end with one last tournament and a big farewell party on Saturday 29th September.

5th October 2018: Our new home at Ive Farm Fields is officially opened!
