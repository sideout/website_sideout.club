Dear Indoor Volleyball Community,

We know how important it is for athletes to stay in shape and healthy both physically and mentally during these challenging times. Unfortunately however, the latest Covid-19 restrictions in London and other parts of England mean that indoor volleyball can no longer be played until further notice.
