Starter Series



Tournaments for Beginner Players!

The SideOut Starter Series is a series of one-day beach volleyball tournaments at both Leyton Beach and Worthing Beach specifically for beginner and improver level players who are starting their journey in competitive beach volleyball or looking for a chance to gain more experience playing against like-minded players of a similar level.

NEXT EVENTS
TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm
UK BEACH TOUR
  • All tournaments are 1-star events on the UK Beach Tour
  • All players must hold a valid UKBT Membership (in addition to a SideOut Membership or Single Event Pass)
FORMAT
  • Men's and women's tournaments run alongside each other
  • Pool play followed by elimination rounds
  • Minimum 3 matches per team per tournament
ENTRY RULES & PROCESS
  • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
  • Registration closes when the tournament is full, or on the Friday before each tournament.
  • Entry is first-come, first-served. Once you have entered and paid, you are in the tournament!
  • All teams will receive an email with full details, schedule and timings by the Friday before each tournament.
PRICES
  • Leyton Beach: £31 per team per tournament
  • Worthing Beach: £30 per team per tournament
