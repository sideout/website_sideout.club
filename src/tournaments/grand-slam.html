UKBT Grand Slam, hosted by SideOut



The UK's best of the best are coming back to Leyton!

Featuring live streaming of matches, prize money for the winners and much more, the UKBT Grand Slam will bring the UK’s best male and female beach volleyball players back to Leyton Beach over the course of an action-packed August bank holiday weekend!

DATES
  • Saturday 28 August – Men's & Women’s day 1
  • Sunday 29 August – Men’s & Women's day 2 (quarterfinals, semis and finals)
TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm
UK BEACH TOUR
  • This is a 5-star event on the UK Beach Tour
  • All players must hold a valid Full UKBT Membership (in addition to a SideOut 365-day Membership or Single Event Pass)
TOURNAMENT FORMAT
  • Up to 16 teams per gender
  • Pool play followed by single elimination
  • 4 pools of 4 teams each
  • 1st ranked in each pool automatically qualifies for quarterfinals
  • 2nd and 3rd ranked in each pool play off against other pools to qualify for quarterfinals
  • Minimum 3 matches per team
NOTE: The format is subject to confirmation and may be adapted by the organisers.
