UKBT Wilson Championships, hosted by SideOut



A brand new series of elite showcase events comes to SideOut Beach in Leyton! These events are 4-star events on the UK Beach Tour.

The UKBT Wilson Championships, hosted by SideOut is a series of elite showcase beach volleyball tournaments taking place at SideOut Beach in Leyton in August and September 2020.

Featuring live streaming of all matches, prize money for the winners and much more, the UKBT Wilson Championships will bring the UK’s best male and female beach volleyball players to SideOut Beach in Leyton over the course of 4 action-packed events!

DATES
  • Saturday 15 August – Women’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Sunday 16 August – Men’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Saturday 22 August – Women’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Sunday 23 August – Men’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Saturday 5 September – Women’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Sunday 6 September – Men’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm
UK BEACH TOUR
  • All tournaments are 4-star events on the UK Beach Tour
  • All players must hold a valid Full UKBT Membership (in addition to a SideOut 365-day Membership or Single Event Pass)
TOURNAMENT FORMAT
  • Up to 12 teams per gender
  • Pool play followed by single elimination
  • 4 pools of 3 teams each
  • 1st ranked in each pool automatically qualifies for quarterfinals
  • 2nd and 3rd ranked in each pool play off against other pools to qualify for quarterfinals
  • Minimum 3 matches per team
NOTE: The format may be adapted if the number of teams in an event is below 12.
PLAYING RULES
  • All matches will be played to a best of 3 sets (15, 15, 11)
  • Must win a set by 2 clear points, with no caps
  • Teams change ends every 7 points in the first two sets and every 5 points in the third set
  • One time-out per team in the first two sets and one extra time-out per team in the 3rd set (so maximum 1 time-out per team in a 2-set match and maximum 2 time-outs per team in a 3-set match)
ENTRY RULES
  • Register and pay online here
  • Registration closes on the Monday before each tournament
  • Participating teams will be determined as set out below and announced on the Tuesday before each tournament
  • Maximum 12 teams per gender
  • If more than 12 entries are received, teams will be ranked by UKBT seeding points (i.e. the top 4 results in each player’s national ranking) and the top 12 ranked entrants will be eligible to participate
  • The winning team from the previous UKBT Wilson Qualifiers tournament will gain automatic qualification to each event.
  • Up to 2 wildcards per gender may be granted in exceptional circumstances and at the organisers’ absolute discretion.
  • If wildcards are granted and/or the winning team of the previous Qualifiers tournament uses their space, only the top 9, 10 or 11 ranked teams will be eligible.
  • Teams that do not get into the tournament will be offered priority entry to the UKBT Wilson Qualifiers 3-star tournament that will take place on the same day.
ENTRY FEE
  • £42 per team per tournament
