All Nations



Represent your country!

VolleyFirst and SideOut are excited to announce that the All Nations Beach Volleyball tournament is back for yet another year!

​

2020 DATES TBC

WHAT'S THE FORMAT?

√  Men's and Women's 2-a-side on Saturday and Sunday

    (Pool play and all matches up to quarterfinals on Saturday, with semis and finals on Sunday)

√  Mixed 2-a-side on Sunday. 

√  Minimum of 4 matches per team, per tournament.

​

HOW DOES IT WORK?

√  For all competitions (men, women and mixed), you must enter as a team.

√  You can enter the single gender, the mixed, or both tournaments. 

√  Entry will be limited to 24 men's, 24 women's and 32 mixed teams.

√  Entry is on a "first-come-first-served" basis.

√ You MUST play with someone from your country, unless there are EXCEPTIONAL circumstances. Playing with someone from a different country will only be allowed at the organisers' discretion.

√  Your entry will not be confirmed until we have received your payment.

​

PLAYING TOPS

√  All players will get an awesome All Nations beach volleyball playing top at no extra cost!

√  Men receive vests, women receive crop tops for the women's tournament and female-cut vests for the mixed tournament. 

√  Once you have entered online, please send a single email to with the following information: 

  • Your team's country;

  • Your and your partner's clothing size (from S to XXXL); 

  • Your and your partner's kit number (one player must wear #1 and the other #2); 

  • Optional: Your name to be printed on the kit (this is optional, at an extra cost of £8 per team, or £4 per player. The extra cost can be paid on the day of the tournament).

​

WHERE IS IT HAPPENING?

√  SideOut Beach, Leyton - London, E10 5HQ.

√  On site facilities include cafe, chill-out area and more! 

​

WHAT ELSE IS HAPPENING?

√  Tournament party on the Saturday night.

√  Delicious food and drinks throughout the weekend. 

​

HOW MUCH IS IT?

√  £42 per team per tournament.
