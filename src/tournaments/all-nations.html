All Nations Beach Volleyball



Represent your country!

VolleyFirst and SideOut are excited to announce that the All Nations Beach Volleyball tournament is returning to Leyton Beach with a bang this year!

For our beach volleyball regulars, the men's, women's and mixed 2-a-side competitions are back again!

Not played much beach volleyball before? Prefer to share the court with more players, or just to play indoor rules on the sand? Our men's and women's 4-a-side tournament has you covered!

2021 DATES
  • Saturday 26 & Sunday 27 June: Men's & Women's 2 vs 2.
    • Semifinals and finals to be played on the Sunday.
  • Sunday 27 June: Mixed 2 vs 2
  • Saturday 17 July: Men's & Women's 4 vs 4
LOCATION
  • Leyton Beach, London
FORMAT & RULES
  • Men's and Women's 2 vs 2:
    • 2-day event.
    • Pool play and all matches up to quarterfinals on Saturday 26 June, with semifinals and finals on Sunday 27 June.
    • Semifinals and finals to be played on the Sunday.
    • Entry limited to 24 men's and 24 women's teams.
    • Beach volleyball rules!
  • Mixed 2 vs 2:
    • 1-day event.
    • All matches on Sunday 27 June.
    • Entry limited to 32 teams.
    • Beach volleyball rules!
  • Men's and Women's 4 vs 4:
    • 1-day event.
    • All matches on Saturday 17 July.
    • Entry limited to 24 men's and 24 women's teams.
    • 2 male and 2 female players on court at all times. Substitutes allowed!
    • Indoor volleyball rules!
  • All tournaments:
    • You must enter as a team.
    • All players on a team must be from the same country. Playing with someone from a different country will only be allowed in exceptional circumstances and at the organisers' discretion!
    • Minimum 4 matches per team per tournament.
    • Times: Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm each day.
    • Detailed schedule and timings will be provided by email no later than the Friday before each event.
PLAYING TOPS
  • All players will get an awesome customised All Nations beach volleyball playing top included with their entry!
  • Men get regular tank-top vests, women get female-cut tank-tops.
  • Once you have entered online, please respond to the confirmation email with the following information:
    • Your team's country;
    • Your and your partner's clothing size (from S to XXL);
    • Your and your partner's kit number (one player must wear #1 and the other #2);
    • Optional: Your name to be printed on the kit (this is optional, at an extra cost of £8 per team, or £4 per player. The extra cost can be paid on the day of the tournament).
HOW TO ENTER
  • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
    • 2 vs 2: All players must have a valid SideOut membership or single event pass.
    • 4 vs 4: Open to non-members!
  • To guarantee a personalised playing top you must enter by:
    • Friday 28th May at 12:00 noon (2 vs 2)
    • Friday 18th June at 12:00 noon (4 vs 4)
  • Entry is first-come, first-served. Once you have entered and paid, you are in the tournament!
  • All teams will receive an email with full details, schedule and timings by the Friday before each event.
HOW MUCH IS IT?
  • Men's and Women's 2 vs 2:
    • £50 per team.
  • Mixed 2 vs 2:
    • £50 per team.
  • Men's and Women's 4 vs 4:
    • £100 per team of 4 players.
    • Substitutes are charged an extra £25 per player.
Team numbers are strictly limited, so get your entry in early to avoid disappointment!
Registration opens 21st May at 12:00 noon!
