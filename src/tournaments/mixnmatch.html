Mix 'n' Match



Mix it up, with this unique format!

Players enter individually. You don't need a partner! On the day, we will draw one male and one female name out of a hat to create mixed teams. Teams then play each other in pool play format. After pool play is finished, we throw everyone back into the hat and draw names again. Everyone gets shuffled around and paired up with a new partner and we start again! Repeat this process a few times and, at the end of the day, the male and female winners of the Mix 'n' Match tournament will be crowned!
