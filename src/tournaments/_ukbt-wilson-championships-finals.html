UKBT Wilson Championships Finals, hosted by SideOut



The best of the best battle it out at the grand finale of the 2020 UKBT Wilson Championships!

The UKBT Wilson Championships Finals, hosted by SideOut in Leyton on 12-13 September 2020, is the culmination of the 2020 UKBT Wilson Championships.

After gathering valuable points across the first 3 tournaments of the UKBT Wilson Championships, the very best male and female teams in the UK will be heading to SideOut Beach for one last action-packed event, which will feature live streaming of matches, prize money and of course the chance to be crowned the 2020 UKBT Wilson Champions!

DATES
  • Sat 12 September: UKBT Wilson Championships Finals, Qualification Tournament (max. 12 teams per gender)
  • Sun 13 September: UKBT Wilson Championships Finals Main Draw (max. 6 teams per gender)
TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm
UK BEACH TOUR
  • The Qualification Tournament is a 3-star tournament and the Main Draw is a 4-star tournament on the UKBT.
  • All players must hold a valid Full UKBT Membership (in addition to a SideOut 365-day Membership or Single Event Pass)
TOURNAMENT FORMAT
  • 6 teams per gender
  • Pool play followed by single elimination
  • 2 pools of 3 teams each
  • 1st ranked in each pool automatically qualifies for semifinals
  • 2nd and 3rd ranked in each pool play off against other pools to qualify for semifinals
  • Minimum 3 matches per team
NOTE: The format may be adapted if the number of teams per gender is below 6.
PLAYING RULES
  • All matches will be played to a best of 3 sets (15, 15, 11)
  • Must win a set by 2 clear points, with no caps
  • Teams change ends every 7 points in the first two sets and every 5 points in the third set
  • One time-out per team in the first two sets and one extra time-out per team in the 3rd set (so maximum 1 time-out per team in a 2-set match and maximum 2 time-outs per team in a 3-set match)
ENTRY RULES
  • Register and pay online here
  • Registration closes at 9:00pm on Monday 7th September.
  • Participating teams will be determined as set out below and announced on Tuesday 8th September.
  • Any UK-based players with a valid Full UKBT Membership can apply for entry.
  • This is a 2-day tournament:
    • Men’s & Women’s Qualification Tournament: Saturday 12 September (Maximum 12 teams per gender)
    • Men’s & Women’s Main Draw: Sunday 13 September (Maximum 6 teams per gender)
  • MAIN DRAW
    • Priority entry to the Main Draw will be determined as follows:
      • Up to 3 spaces will be reserved for the winning teams of the previous UKBT Wilson Championships events.*
      • Between 2-5 spaces** will be determined by each team’s total points accumulated only from the previous UKBT Wilson Championships events (points gained from other UKBT events this season, international events or from any events last season will not count).
      • All other teams will compete in the Qualification Tournament on Saturday 12 September (see below for details).
      • 1 space in the Main Draw will be reserved for the winning team of the Qualification Tournament. ***
      * In order to automatically qualify to the Main Draw of the Finals, the team that registers for the Finals must be the exact same team that won one of the first 3 events. In other words, players who won an event together must not change partners for the Finals if they wish to gain automatic qualification. If they do change partners, their team’s reserved space will be released and their qualification will be based on the same criteria as all the other teams.
      ** The final number (minimum 2, maximum 5) of available spaces based on total points will depend on the number of teams that gain automatic qualification by winning the previous 3 tournaments. The possible scenarios are:
      Previous Event Winners Saturday Qualification Winner Spaces available based on points
      Scenario A 3 1 2
      Scenario B 2 1 3
      Scenario C 1 1 4
      Scenario D 0 1 5

      *** The team that wins the Qualification Tournament on Saturday 12 September must be the exact same team that participates in the Main Draw on Sunday 13 September. If a team is unable to participate, then the next-placed team will qualify instead.
  • QUALIFICATION TOURNAMENT
    • The Qualification Tournament will be open to a maximum of 12 teams per gender. If there are more teams entered, priority of entry will be determined as follows:
      • 1 space will be reserved for the winning team from the previous UKBT Wilson Qualifiers tournament on 5/6 September.
      • 11 spaces will be determined by each team’s UKBT seeding points (i.e. the top 4 results in each player’s national ranking).
  • SEEDING POLICY
    • Once all the teams are determined in the Qualification Tournament and the Main Draw based on the above criteria, they will be re-seeded in accordance with the normal UKBT seeding system for 2020, i.e. total points from each player's best 4 finishes from all events in 2019 and 2020.
ENTRY FEE
  • £42 per team per tournament
