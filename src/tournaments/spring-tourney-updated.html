SideOut Spring Tourney



Are you an old schooler and missing the good old days of beach volleyball?

 

Or maybe you're too young to remember, but want to experience what it used to be like?

​

Sideout scoring and epic comebacks, one-set matches to 15 points (which could sometimes take more than a whole hour!), serves can't touch the net, no antennas, double elimination format, beer and cash prizes - the list goes on...

 

The SideOut Old School Tourney has it all! * 

​

Old school shorts, swimwear, tops, hats, visors, sunglasses and balls are strongly encouraged but completely optional! 

 

This one is "﻿For the old to remember and for the new to learn!" 

​

See you on the sand!

(* Except for the larger courts!)

FORMAT​​ & RULES

  • Everything Old School!

    • double elimination tournament format (play until you lose 2 matches)

    • ​​sideout scoring (only score a point after your team serves)

    • serves can't touch the net

    • no antennas (ball must be played over the net between the posts!)

  • Winners get cash and beers!

  • Old school gear encouraged but optional!
