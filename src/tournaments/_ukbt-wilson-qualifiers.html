UKBT Wilson Qualifiers, hosted by SideOut



A chance to be part of the UKBT Wilson Championships, compete and earn extra UKBT ranking points!

The UKBT Wilson Qualifiers, hosted by SideOut is a tournament set up especially for any teams that did not qualify for the main draw of the UKBT Wilson Championships and any other teams that want to enjoy high level competition!

These tournaments are 3-star events on the UK Beach Tour and the winning teams will get guaranteed entry into the next UKBT Wilson Championships 4-star event

DATES
  • Saturday 15 August – Women’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Sunday 16 August – Men’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Saturday 22 August – Women’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Sunday 23 August – Men’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Saturday 5 September – Women’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
  • Sunday 6 September – Men’s tournament (max. 12 teams)
TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm
UK BEACH TOUR
  • All tournaments are 3-star events on the UK Beach Tour
  • All players must hold a valid Full UKBT Membership (in addition to a SideOut 365-day Membership or Single Event Pass)
  • The first placed male and female teams on weekends 1 and 2 will get guaranteed entry into the UKBT Wilson Championships 4-star event the following weekend!
  • The first placed male and female teams on weekend 3 will get guaranteed entry into the UKBT Wilson Championships Finals QUALIFICATION TOURNAMENT (3-star) the following weekend!
    IMPORTANT:
    The guaranteed entry must be used at the very next event by the exact same pairing that wins the UKBT Wilson Qualifier tournament.
    The winners of the Qualifiers tournament on weekend 3 will NOT receive guaranteed entry to the UKBT Wilson Championship Finals Main Draw on 13 September, but to the Qualification Tournament on 12 September.
PLAYING RULES
  • All matches will be played to a best of 3 sets (15, 15, 11)
  • Must win a set by 2 clear points, with no caps
  • Teams change ends every 7 points in the first two sets and every 5 points in the third set
  • One time-out per team in the first two sets and one extra time-out per team in the 3rd set (so maximum 1 time-out per team in a 2-set match and maximum 2 time-outs per team in a 3-set match)
ENTRY RULES
  • Register and pay online here
  • Priority entry is given to teams that entered the same weekend’s 4-star event but did not make the main draw. Registration for the remaining spaces will be on a first-come, first-served basis and will open after entries for the 4-star event are closed.
ENTRY FEE
  • £42 per team per tournament
