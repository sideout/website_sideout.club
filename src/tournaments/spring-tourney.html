SideOut Spring Tourney



A great way to see in the new season!

The SideOut Spring Tourney is the perfect way to welcome the new season and get your sand legs back!

The event takes place over a full weekend with men's and women's tournaments on the Saturday and a mixed tournament on the Sunday. Players can choose to play one tournament only (men's, women's or mixed) or they can enter both the men's/women's and the mixed!

See you on the sand!

2019 DATES
  • SideOut Beach, Leyton
    • Saturday 27 April 2019: Men's and Women's Tournaments
    • Sunday 28 April 2019: Mixed Tournament
  • SideOut Beach, Worthing:
    • Saturday 27 April 2019: Men's and Women's Tournaments
​
TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm
  • Exact times to be confirmed before each tournament
​
FORMAT​
  • Pool play followed by elimination rounds
  • Minimum 4 matches per team per tournament

​​

PRICE
  • Saturday: £34 per team
  • Sunday: £32 per team 
  • Play both days and get £8 off your mixed tournament entry!
    (Discount is automatically applied at payment stage if the player registering the mixed team has already entered the men's or women's tournament.)
