SideOut Challenger Series



Tournaments for Intermediate Players!

The SideOut Challenger Series is a series of one-day beach volleyball tournaments aimed specifically at intermediate level players who are looking for a chance to gain more competitive experience playing against like-minded players of a similar level.

Each event is a 2-star event on the UK Beach Tour and features a men's and a women's 2-a-side tournament running alongside each other at our venues in London and Worthing. See you on the sand!
