SideOut Grand Prix



An Elite Series in London and the South Coast

The SideOut Grand Prix is a series of elite beach volleyball tournaments taking place in London and Worthing throughout the summer.

Featuring full international rules, prizes and much more, the SideOut Grand Prix is a showcase of top level men's and women's beach volleyball.

Key features:

  • 4-star events on the UK Beach Tour
  • 1-day events in Worthing
  • 2-day events in Leyton
  • Entry determined by UKBT points
  • Cash, prizes and medals for the top 3 teams

UK BEACH TOUR
  • All tournaments are 4-star events on the UK Beach Tour.
  • All players must hold a valid UKBT Membership (in addition to a SideOut Membership or Single Event Pass).
FORMAT
  • Leyton Beach:
    • 2-day events.
    • Pool play, followed by single elimination rounds.
    • Quarterfinals, semifinals and finals played on day 2.
    • Full international scoring (21-21-15) in all matches.
    • Minimum 3 matches per team.
  • Worthing Beach:
    • 1-day events.
    • Pool play, followed by single elimination rounds.
    • Reduced scoring (15-15-11) in pool play.
    • Full international scoring (21-21-15) in elimination rounds.
    • Minimum 3 matches per team.
ENTRY RULES & PROCESS
  • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
  • Registration closes on the Monday before each tournament.
  • Maximum 16 teams per gender (12 for events in Worthing).
  • If more than 16 entries are received, teams will be ranked by UKBT points and the top 16 ranked entrants will be eligible to participate. All other teams will be placed on the reserve list and have their entry fee refunded if they do not get into the tournament.
  • In case of a tie in UKBT points, previous results in SideOut tournaments may also be taken into consideration.
  • Up to 2 wildcards per gender may be granted in exceptional circumstances and at SideOut's absolute discretion. If wildcards are granted, only the top 14 or 15 ranked teams (depending on number of wildcards) will be eligible.
  • All teams will receive an email by the Tuesday before each tournament informing them if they are in the tournament or not.
  • Detailed schedule and timings will be emailed to all teams no later than the Friday before each event.
