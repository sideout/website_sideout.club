Family Open Sessions



Fun on the sand for the whole family!

Want to enjoy some sport, fitness and quality time with your kids in a fun and relaxing environment? Or maybe the kids already train with us and want to get some extra games while you enjoy some downtime?

Let us introduce our brand new Family Open Sessions!

Sessions run from 4:30pm-6:30pm every Friday afternoon from April to September at Leyton Beach.

Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Kids can simply turn up alone or with their family. There's no need to pre-register!

  • Step 2: Pay £3 per child or just £6 for a family of up to 4.

  • Step 3: Just play! All ages and levels are welcome! Courts are available from 4:30pm to 6:30pm.

Please note that these sessions are supervised but not coached, so please ensure that younger kids are accompanied at all times.

See you on the sand!
