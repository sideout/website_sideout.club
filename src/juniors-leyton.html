2021 Junior Programme - Leyton

Beach volleyball is not only a fun sport that is played recreationally on beaches all over the world, but it is also a unique, dynamic and incredibly popular Olympic sport. Since the iconic 2012 Olympic beach volleyball tournament at Horse Guards Parade, the sport has been rapdily growing here in the UK too and the future is bright for youngsters keen to get involved - whether or not they have ever played before!

The SideOut Junior programme is managed and delivered by a highly experienced team of coaches and our goal is to provide top quality training and playing opportunities for boys and girls of all age groups, abilities and levels of experience in a fun, active and safe environment.

We offer training and playing opportunities for boys and girls aged 6-18, as well as sand-based activities for toddlers aged 3-5, all at Leyton Beach.

  • Our 6-18 year olds train on Monday afternoons from 5:00pm and are separated into groups based on age and ablity.
  • Our toddler sessions run on Friday mornings from 10:00am, when parents and carers will also have the option to join in some fitness sessions on the sand!

We also run the SideOut Junior Series, which is a series of tournaments for any young athletes who want to test their skills in a fun yet competitive environment. Check out the Events Calendar and click on the red "Juniors" button to find out what we have on offer throughout the season!

Junior Training, Leyton: Frequently Asked Questions


WHEN WILL I TRAIN?

If you sign up for Open Group Training or Private Group Training, you will train twice per week at Leyton Beach. All groups will train on Tuesdays & Thursdays, with sessions at 18:30-20:15 on one day and at 20:15-22:00 on the other day.

The exact schedule for all groups will be determined as the groups are finalised after the first few sessions of the season.

WHAT DOES IT COST?

- Open Group Training: £280 per person.

- Private Group Training: £2,360 per private group of max. 8 people*.

* A private group can be made up of any number of people up to a maximum of 8, but the total price for the group will always be £2,360. If you would like to have more than 8 people in your private group, please drop us a line!

All players must also have a valid Club Membership, which costs £22 per person and is valid for 365 days.

HOW CAN I PAY?

Training must be paid for by bank transfer.

To pay by bank transfer: transfer the full amount to the SideOut Beach Volleyball Club bank account (details on the registration page) and email us to confirm payment has been made.

If you would like to discuss flexible payment options, please feel free to email us!

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

After you've signed up and paid for training, we will contact you directly by email to give you further information about your training sessions.
