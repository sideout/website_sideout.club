2020 Season Update (1 August 2020)

Tournaments are back!

The health, safety and well-being of our members and our community remains our top priority. We are therefore complying in full with the latest rules and guidance issued by the UK Government and Volleyball England.

This means that all of our events will allow a maximum of 30 persons, including players, organisers and officials and that all persons must maintain a social distance of 2 metres at all times when off the court or when on the court and the ball is not in play.

Train Smart, Play Safe!
