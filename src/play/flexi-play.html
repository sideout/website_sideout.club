Flexi Play



Book & play at Leyton Beach!

Are you a member of our Club? Do you want to pre-book some playing time for you and your friends? Flexi Play is just right for you!

THE DETAILS

  • Sessions are available to book at any of the following days and times (subject to court availability):

    - April to July: Wednesdays between 6:00-10:00pm

    - August & September: Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays between 6:00-10:00pm

  • All sessions must be used by 26th September 2022

    - 48-hour cancellation policy applies for each booked session

  • Max. up to 8 players per group

    - All participants must have a valid Club Membership

  • All sessions are 2 hours long and are not coached

  • Price: £32 per 2-hour session

    - Discounts are available for blocks of 5 (£30 per session) or blocks of 10 (£28 per session)
