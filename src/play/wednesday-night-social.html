Side-it-Out

Would you like plenty of court time with many different opponents? Want to compete but prefer to avoid committing to advance tournament registration? Worried about getting cold between matches? Read on because we've got you fully covered!

Side-it-Out offers players non-stop game-play by bringing together our popular All-You-Can-Play with the ultra-fast paced King of the Court competition format!

Here's how it works:

  • Find a partner! We recommend coming with a playing partner. If you don’t have one, let us know and we will do our best to link you up with someone in advance or on the day. You can change partners from week to week, but you will play all your games with the same partner on each day.

  • Turn up from 18:30 to sign up on the spot, pay your entry fees and start warming up.

  • Games start at 19:00. You will get at least 3 rounds of non-stop play ending at around 20:30. All levels are welcome and all players will be accommodated!

  • Collect ranking points and win great prizes! You and your partner will win Side-it-Out ranking points for every round you play on each day and there will be some cool goodies up for grabs for the top ranked players and teams at the end of each month!

So, what exactly is King of the Court?

An exciting, fast and furious-paced tournament format taking the beach volleyball world by storm!

The format sees up to 5 teams play against each other on a single court, until one men's team is crowned King of the Court and one women’s team is crowned Queen of the Court.

If you have ever played the classic "king of the court" or "queen of the court" in training, you will know how this works. In short, the objective is to get to the "king" or "queen" side of the court and stay there for as long as possible by siding out and accumulating points! After each rally, the losing team leaves the court, with the winning team moving to, or staying on, the "king" or "queen" side.

Check out www.kingofthecourt.com/game for more info on how it works.


Mini-Tournaments

Ever wished you could play in tournaments without the long waits between matches or without spending the whole day on the sand? Or maybe you just wished you could play mid-week tournaments to give you some extra competition or simply to keep your precious weekends free? If you answered yes to any of the above, our Wednesday Night Mini-Tournaments are for you!

Here's how it works:

  • Find a partner! You need to enter each Mini-Tournament as a team. If you don’t have a partner, let us know in advance and we will do our best to link you up with someone.

  • Register! Spaces are strictly limited to ensure lots of game play. Members of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club can pre-register online here pre-register online here to guarantee their entry in advance. Members and non-members can also just turn up and sign up on the spot, but entry will be ‘first-come, first-served’ and subject to availability on the day.

  • Arrive from 6:00pm to sign in and start warming up. Entry fees are just £18 per team if both players have a valid SideOut Beach Volleyball Club Membership , or £32 per team (£16 per player) if one or both players do not have a membership.

  • Games start at 6:30pm. You will get at least 3 matches and play will end no later than 10pm.. All levels are welcome and all players will be accommodated!

  • Win prizes and goodies! The winners of each Mini-Tournament will win various cool prizes and goodies!
