Wednesday Night Social



Want to just play with friends after work? Want to meet new people to play with? Or just want to turn up and play without the commitment of pre-registering in advance? Either way, our Wednesday Night Socials have got you covered!

The courts are available from 6:30pm every Wednesday evening and on various other weeknights throughout the season at Leyton Beach. Simply check the events calendar to find out exactly when!

In case you didn't already know, here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Turn up at any time during the evening - alone or with friends. No need to pre-register!

  • Step 2: Pay £12 per person or just £6 if you are a registered member of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club.

    Please present your Club membership card (downloadable as a PDF from our registration system).

  • Step 3: Just play! All levels are welcome! Courts are available from 6:30pm to 10:00pm.

Side-it-Out

Non-stop game-play!

Introducing Side-it-Out, a new exciting format which brings together our popular All-You-Can-Play with the ultra-fast paced All-You-Can-PlayKing of the Court competition format!

Want to get lots of court time with lots of different opponents? Want to compete but prefer to avoid committing to advance tournament registration? Worried about getting cold between matches? All-You-Can-PlaySide-it-Out has you fully covered!

Here's how it works:

  • Find a partner! We recommend coming with a playing partner. If you don’t have one, let us know and we will do our best to link you up with someone in advance or on the day. You can change partners from week to week, but you will play all your games with the same partner on each day.

  • Turn up from 18:30 to sign up on the spot, pay your entry fees and start warming up.

  • Games start at 19:00. You will get at least 3 rounds of non-stop play ending at around 20:30. All levels are welcome and all players will be accommodated!

  • Collect ranking points and win great prizes! You and your partner will win Side-it-Out ranking points for every round you play on each day and there will be some cool goodies up for grabs for the top ranked players and teams at the end of each month!


See you on the sand!
