Beginners' GameTime



Looking to try out beach volleyball in a relaxed setting or not sure where to start when it comes to giving beach volleyball a try? Our Beginners' GameTime is perfect for those who are new to the sport. This beginners' session is open to all and provides the opportunity to practice, play and compete in a welcoming, no pressure environment.

Sessions run from 6:45pm-8:30pm every Wednesday afternoon from April to September at Leyton Beach.

Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Simply turn up - either alone or with friends. No need to pre-register!

  • Step 2: Pay £10 per person or just £5 if you are a registered member of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club. Please present your Club membership card (downloadable as a PDF from our registration system). Sessions are free for members who have also signed up for Beginners’ Training!

  • Step 3: Just play! Remember all levels are welcome and our coaches will be available to facilitate.

Please note that these sessions are supervised but not coached,

See you on the sand!
