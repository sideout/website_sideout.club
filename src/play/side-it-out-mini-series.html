Side-it-Out Mini-Series



Non-stop game-play to kick off the new season!

Introducing a new and exciting take on our popular Side-it-Out competition format!

Want to start off the new season by getting plenty of court time with lots of different partners and opponents? Want to get some games in a fun, relaxed environment while meeting lots of new people? The Side-it-Out Mini-Series is just right for you!

The Side-it-Out Mini-Series will run on Saturday the 2nd, Saturday the 16th and Saturday the 23rd of April 2022 at 11:00am-4:00pmat Leyton Beach.

Here's what you need to know:

  • 1: No partner needed! You will change partners throughout each day and track your points individually.

  • 2: Members of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club can pre-register online here to get priority on the day. Members and non-members can also just turn up and sign up on the day.

  • 3: Arrive from 10:30am to sign in and start warming up.Entry fees are £14 per person or just £8 if you have a valid SideOut Beach Volleyball Club Membership .

  • 5: Collect ranking points and win great prizes! You will win ranking points for every day you play and there will be some cool goodies up for grabs for the top ranked players at the end of each day and again at the end of the series!

So, how exactly does this work?

The Side-it-Out Mini-Series sees up to 8 players play with and against each other on a single court and collect points every time they win a rally by siding out.

If you have ever played the classic "king of the court" or "queen of the court" in training, you will know how this works. In short, the objective is to get to the "king" or "queen" side of the court and stay there for as long as possible by siding out and accumulating points!

There’s a twist though! For this version you gain points as an individual instead of as a team. After each rally, the player that made the error leaves the court, with the remaining players rotating towards, or staying on, the "king" or "queen" side. This way, you will change partners frequently and get to play lots of beach volleyball with and against many different players!
