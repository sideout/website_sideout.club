Open Play



Evening play at Leyton Beach!

Want to just play with friends after work? Want to meet new people to play with? Or just want to turn up and play without the commitment of pre-registering in advance? Either way, our Open Play evenings have got you covered!

Open Play is available from 6:30pm every Friday evening and on various other weeknights throughout the season at Leyton Beach. Simply check the events calendar to find out exactly when!

In case you didn't already know, here's how it works:

- Step 1: Turn up at any time during the evening - alone or with friends. No need to pre-register!

- Step 2: Pay £10 per person or just £5 if you are a registered member of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club. Please present your Club membership card (downloadable as a PDF from our registration system). We accept cash or card payments.

- Step 3: Just play! All levels are welcome! Courts are available from 6:30pm to 10:00pm.

See you on the sand!
