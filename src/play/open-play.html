Open Play



Evening play at Leyton Beach!

Want to just play with friends after work? Want to meet new people to play with? Or just want to turn up and play without the commitment of pre-registering in advance? Either way, our Open Play evenings have got you covered!

Open Play is available from 6:30pm every Wednesday and Friday evening and on various other weeknights throughout the season at Leyton Beach. Simply check the events calendar to find out exactly when!

In case you didn't already know, here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Turn up at any time during the evening - alone or with friends. No need to pre-register!

  • Step 2: Make your payment:

    - £11 per person if you are not a Club Member.

    - Only £5 per person if you present a valid Club Membership card (downloadable as a PDF from our registration system once you have become a member).

    - Fridays are FREE if you have also signed up for Open Group Training, Private Group Training or Beginners’ Training!

  • Step 3: Just play! All levels are welcome! Courts are available from 6:30pm to 10:00pm.

See you on the sand!
