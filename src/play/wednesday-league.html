Coast Café Wednesday League



SideOut is proud to present the brand new Coast Café Wednesday League, taking place at SideOut Beach in Worthing. A different way to enjoy some games and compete in a friendly environment!

Starting on the 6th of April and running until the end of September 2022, the Coast Café Wednesday League will offer different formats every month throughout the season.

April: Mixed 2s
May: Men’s 2s
June: Women’s 2s
July: Junior 2s
August: Mixed 4s
September: Junior 4s

A maximum of 8 teams will be able to enter each monthly league. Games will be played every Wednesday between 6pm and 10pm at SideOut Beach in Worthing, with each team playing 2 games per evening capped off by playoff matches to determine the final ranking on the final day of each league!

FORMAT

We will be using a new format that ensures more action for everyone! All games will be played to 3 sets of 21 points (2 points clear), regardless whether a team has already won the first two sets or not. Each set will count for 1 league point, meaning that a team could earn 1, 2 or 3 league points towards their final ranking from every game played.

Refereeing:

The games will be refereed by other teams from the previous or following games. Otherwise, if both of the playing teams agree before their game, they will be allowed to self-referee, calling their own faults. We hope that this will develop and promote a spirit of fair play and create a healthier competitive environment.

Schedule:

A schedule will be sent to all teams before the start of their monthly league and teams must then ensure that they attend at the right times each Wednesday.

A typical schedule will be:
1st Wednesday: 2 games per team
2nd Wednesday: 2 games per team
3rd Wednesday: 2 games per team
4th Wednesday: 1 game per team + 1 playoff game

Substitutions and replacements:

  • 2 vs 2:

    If for any reason one of the players cannot play, a replacement player will be allowed for no more than 2 games and there will be an extra charge for the replacement player. No in-game substitutions are allowed.

  • 4 vs 4:

    Up to 2 substitutes will be allowed to enter the competition in advance at an extra cost (6 players in total per team). Unlimited in-game substitutions are allowed. Each team must have at least 1 player of each gender on court at all times.

Prices:

  • 2s: £34 per player (£68 per team) + £10 per replacement player (replacement players can play a maximum of 2 games)

  • 4s: £20 per player (£80 per team) + £15 per extra substitute player (maximum of 2 substitutes allowed, for a total of 6 players per team)

  • Junior 2s: £16.50 per player (£33 per team) + £8 per replacement player (replacement players can play a maximum of 3 games)

  • Junior 4s (mixed and non-mixed): £10 per player (£40 per team) + £8 per extra substitute player (maximum of 2 substitutes allowed, for a total of 6 players per team)

Prizes:

Lots of prizes to be won, including meal and drink vouchers courtesy of our friends at Coast Café, SideOut goodies and free beach volleyball play!

Registration:

  • Register and pay online via our registration page.

  • If you would like to enter substitute players, please send us an email on info[at]sideout.co.uk and we will let you know how to proceed.

  • Registration closes when the league is full.

  • Entry is first-come, first-served. Once you have entered and paid, your team is in the league!

  • All teams will receive an email with full details, schedule and timings before the start of each monthly league.

  • If you need to use a replacement player, please let us know. Payment for the replacement player must be made before starting the game, either at the venue or online in advance.

Cancellations and refunds:

When signing up, a team commits to play the whole month, so it is important to make sure your team is available. We would also recommend having a back-up option as cover in case a member of your team becomes unavailable for a game or two.

SideOut’s refund and cancellation policy applies.
