Bespoke training camps in southern Greece

Following the success of our annual training camps in Rhodes, Athens and Figueira da Foz and having listened carefully to the feedback of the beach volleyball community, we are very excited to announce these brand new and unique SideOut beach volleyball camps!

​

Tell me more!  These are fully bespoke, highly focused training camps which are ideal for small groups that want top quality coaching and excellent playing opportunities customised specifically for their needs - all in the gorgeous city of Kalamata in the southern Peloponnese region of Greece.

​

How does it work?   In short, you get to build your own camp according to your own needs! You create your own training groups, decide how much  (or even how little!) you want to train and play, what you want to work on and the dates you would like to attend - and leave all the rest to us and our local partners at Kalamata Beach Volley. Whatever you may want or need,  just ask and we can probably make it happen! 

​

How many people do we need?  We would recommend that each training group is made up of 6 players. However, we require a minimum of 4 players and a maximum of 8 players per group. We can accommodate a maximum of 4 training groups at any time.

​

When can we go?  You are most welcome at any time of the year! We would recommend going between the months of April and October, but bear in mind that July and August are peak season, so availability could be limited and prices could be higher.  

​

What's included?  It's completely up to you! To help get you started, we have created the following Base Package (which is of course fully adaptable to your preferences):

​

  • 7 nights' stay at a seaside hotel, 10 mins walk from the courts; 

  • Breakfast at the hotel each morning;

  • Lunch at the court-side cafe each afternoon; 

  • 11 training sessions over 8 days;

  • Fully customisable training according to the needs of your group;

  • Tournament with local teams and prizes for top teams;

  • Plenty of free game play (including under floodlights);

  • Access to physiotherapist;

  • Airport transfers (to and from Kalamata airport only); and

  • 1 playing top per player. 

​(Flights are not included.)

​

*** Check out an example schedule for this Base Package and the prices for this package on the right hand side of this page! ***  

​

What else?  If you would like anything else added to your package such as sightseeing, guided tours, experiences (or even extra training!), just ask and we will do our best to take care of it for you! 

​

Who's coaching?  Your coaches will be Greek ex-international athletes George Koulieris and Manos Xenakis, both of whom have had illustrious careers both on the sand and indoors.  They will be joined by additional coaches from SideOut's coaching team. 

​

What about the location?  Kalamata has a long tradition in volleyball and beach volleyball, with many of Greece's top players coming from the city. Our partners' purpose-built facilities include two courts with top quality sand and equipment, while their excellent floodlighting means that the courts are available all day and night. You are also never far from a drink or snack in the adjacent beach bar and cafe or a refreshing swim just a few yards away in the Mediterranean! 

​

How do we get there?  There are direct flights to Kalamata from London and other major European cities, or you can fly there via Athens or Thessaloniki.  Alternatively Kalamata is only a 2 1/2 hour drive from Athens Airport, which offers more flight options from more cities. 

​

We're in! How do we sign up?  Firstly, please drop us a line to let us know your preferred dates and the likely number of people in your group(s).  We will then let you know availability and, once all the basic details are agreed, we will confirm your final price and provide you with full details for signing up. 

​

We look forward to seeing you in Kalamata!
