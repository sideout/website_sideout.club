Worthing Court Bookings: Frequently Asked Questions


HOW DO I BOOK A COURT?

It's simple! All you need to do is go to the bookings calendar on this page and create a short and simple user profile. Note that, if you only have a user profile on our events registration system, you will also need a separate profile here for booking a court. Once you have this, simply choose the date and time you want to start your booking, choose your preferred booking duration and click 'create booking'. This will take you straight to the payment page.

WHAT DOES IT COST?

Members of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club pay £16 per hour. Other users pay £21 per hour. (Membership is only £22 per year and is available here). We only accept payment through PayPal, which offers maximum flexibility and ensures that your payments are secure! No additional charges apply and you do not need a PayPal account to make payment.

WHAT DOES THE PRICE INCLUDE?

Our prices include access to all on-site facilities as well as the use of one ball on your court. These prices do not including coaching or other services and do not apply to corporate bookings or other similar events; for such details please contact us via the form at the bottom of this page.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

Once you've paid, your booking will appear on the online calendar and you will receive a confirmation email with additional instructions. That's it - session booked! Just turn up at the time you've booked and enjoy your time on the sand!

Please note that if you do not make payment within 15 minutes, your booking will be automatically deleted from the system.

REFUNDS? BAD WEATHER?

All bookings are non-refundable. However, if you cancel at least 24 hours before the start of your booking, we will give you a credit towards a future booking. We are open come rain or shine, but if particularly bad weather prevents play on the day of your booking, we will also provide you with a credit for a future booking.
