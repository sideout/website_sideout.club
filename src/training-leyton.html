Leyton Training: Frequently Asked Questions


WHEN WILL I TRAIN?

If you sign up for Open Group Training or Private Group Training, you will train twice per week at Leyton Beach. All groups will train on Tuesdays & Thursdays, with sessions at 18:30-20:15 on one day and at 20:15-22:00 on the other day.

If you sign up for Beginners' Training, you will train once per week at Leyton Beach. All groups will train on Mondays, with sessions at 18:45-20:30.

The exact schedule for all groups will be determined as the groups are finalised after the first few sessions of the season.

WHAT DOES IT COST?

- Open Group Training: £280 per person.

- Private Group Training: £2,360 per private group of max. 8 people*.

- Beginners' Training: £55 per person for each calendar month.

* A private group can be made up of any number of people up to a maximum of 8, but the total price for the group will always be £2,360. If you would like to have more than 8 people in your private group, please drop us a line!

All players must also have a valid Club Membership, which costs £22 per person and is valid for 365 days.

HOW CAN I PAY?

Training must be paid for by bank transfer.

To pay by bank transfer: transfer the full amount to the SideOut Beach Volleyball Club bank account (details on the registration page) and email us to confirm payment has been made.

If you would like to discuss flexible payment options, please feel free to email us!

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

After you've signed up and paid for training, we will contact you directly by email to give you further information about your training sessions.
