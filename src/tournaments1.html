SideOut King & Queen of the court

in Causes by Tom Phillips 3 Comments

The objective is to get to the "king" or "queen" side of the court and stay there for as long as possible by siding out and accumulating points! After each rally, the losing team leaves the court, with the winning team moving to, or staying on, the "king" or "queen" side.

Mix 'n' Match

in Causes by Tom Phillips 3 Comments

Players enter individually. You don't need a partner! On the day, we will draw one male and one female name out of a hat to create mixed teams. Teams then play each other in pool play format. After pool play is finished, we throw everyone back into the hat and draw names again. Everyone gets shuffled around and paired up with a new partner and we start again! Repeat this process a few times and, at the end of the day, male and female winners of each Mix 'n' Match tournament will be crowned!
