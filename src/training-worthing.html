Summer 2022 Training - Worthing

This season at SideOut Beach in Worthing, we are running a new full training programme for all ages and skill levels, including Junior and Beginner training on Mondays, Private Group training on Tuesdays, Drop-in sessions on Thursdays and Flexi Training Packages which are designed to suit your group’s schedule and needs.

Below are all your training options followed by FAQs!

A. BEGINNERS' TRAINING

  • Sign up individually and we allocate you to a group

  • 1 coached session per week

    - Every Monday from 18th April to 19th September 2021

    - Times: 20:00-21:45

    - 1hr 30mins coached + 15mins free play

  • Max. up to 10 players per group

    - All participants must have a valid Club Membership

  • Discounted SideOut playing top

  • £49 per person, per calendar month


B. DROP-IN SESSIONS

  • 1 coached session per week

    - Every Thursday (21st April – 22nd September)

    - One session at 18:15-20:00

    - 1hr 30mins coached + 15mins free play

    - All participants must have a valid Club Membership

    - Every Friday from April to September

  • Discounted SideOut playing top

  • £12 per player


C. PRIVATE GROUP TRAINING

  • Create your own training group and choose your coach

    - Subject to coach availability

  • 1 coached session per week

    - Every Tuesday (19th April – 31st July)

    - One session at either 18:15-20:00 or 20:00–21:45

    - 1hr 30mins coached + 15mins free play

  • Max. up to 8 players per group

    - All participants must have a valid Club Membership

  • Free play every Tuesday in August and September during your regular training slot.

  • Free SideOut playing top

  • £1,200 per group

  • Create your own training group and access ‘pay as you go’ training on whichever days and at whatever times you like

Worthing Training: Frequently Asked Questions


WHEN WILL I TRAIN?

In short, whenever and as often as you want - but subject to court and coach availability!

WHAT DOES IT COST?

- 1 session: £75

- Package of 5 sessions: £325 (£65 per session)

- Package of 10 sessions: £600 (£60 per session)

* A training group can be made up of any number of people up to a maximum of 8, but the total price for the group will always be the same.

All players must also have a valid Club Membership, which costs £22 per person and is valid for 365 days.

HOW CAN I PAY?

Training must be paid for by bank transfer.

To pay by bank transfer: transfer the full amount to the SideOut Beach Volleyball Club bank account (details on the registration page) and email us to confirm payment has been made.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

After you've signed up and paid for training, we will contact you directly by email to give you further information about your training sessions.
