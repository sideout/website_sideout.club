New for 2022, we have partnered with Befit@home to offer our training members the best possible physical preparation and fitness training for the beach volleyball season! The package includes 5 x 45-minute online pre-season fitness sessions at a special discounted price for all our training members with the option to carry on throughout the season. There is also a bonus 30-minute full-body stretching and foam rolling video included! Check out this free introductory session to get you going!

Leyton Training: Frequently Asked Questions


WHEN EXACTLY WILL I TRAIN?

The final schedule for all Open Group Training and Private Group Training will be determined as the groups are finalised after the first few sessions of the season. We will be in touch by email to confirm your exact schedule ASAP.

If you sign up for a Flexi Training Package, please contact us ASAP to let us know the days and times that you would like to use your sessions. We will then confirm availability of courts and coaches and come back to you to finalise your sessions.

WHAT DOES IT COST?

- Beginners' Training: £49 per person for each calendar month (except April which costs £25)

- Private Group Training: £1,200 per private group of max. 8 people*.

- Drop-in Training: £12 per person per session.

A private group can be made up of any number of people up to a maximum of 8, but the total price for the group will always be £1,200. If you would like to have more than 8 people in your private group, please drop us a line!

All players must also have a valid Club Membership, which costs £22 per person and is valid for 365 days.

HOW CAN I PAY?

All training packages must be paid for by bank transfer.

To pay by bank transfer: transfer the full amount to the SideOut Beach Volleyball Club bank account (details on the registration page) and email us to confirm payment has been made.

BAD WEATHER?

We are open come rain or shine, but if particularly bad weather prevents play on the day of your training, we will then provide you with a new date for your missed session.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

After you've signed up and paid for training, we will contact you directly by email to give you further information about your training sessions.
