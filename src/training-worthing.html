2021 Training - Worthing

This year at SideOut Beach in Worthing, we are offering flexible “pay as you go” training packages which allow you to train as often as you like throughout the season.

We have the following training packages available:

  • 1 session: £75

  • 5 sessions: £325 (£65 per session)

  • 10 sessions: £600 (£60 per session)

THE DETAILS

  • Create your own training group and train on whichever days and whatever times you like

    - Subject to coach and court availability

  • All sessions must be used by 24th September 2021

    - 48 hour cancellation policy applies for each booked session

  • Max. up to 8 players per group

    - All participants must have a valid 365-day Club Membership

  • Sessions are 1 hour and 45 minutes each

    - 1hr 30mins coached + 15mins free play

Train Smart Play Safe!

The health, safety and well-being of our members and our community is our top priority. All our training sessions will therefore be fully compliant with the rules and guidance issued by the UK Government and Volleyball England.


Worthing Training: Frequently Asked Questions


WHEN WILL I TRAIN?

In short, whenever and as often as you want - but subject to court and coach availability!

WHAT DOES IT COST?

- 1 session: £75

- Package of 5 sessions: £325 (£65 per session)

- Package of 10 sessions: £600 (£60 per session)

* A training group can be made up of any number of people up to a maximum of 8, but the total price for the group will always be the same.

All players must also have a valid Club Membership, which costs £22 per person and is valid for 365 days.

HOW CAN I PAY?

Training must be paid for by bank transfer.

To pay by bank transfer: transfer the full amount to the SideOut Beach Volleyball Club bank account (details on the registration page) and email us to confirm payment has been made.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

After you've signed up and paid for training, we will contact you directly by email to give you further information about your training sessions.
