All-You-Can-Play



Beach volleyball all day long!

Everyone’s favourite day of beach volleyball at SideOut Beach in Leyton and SideOut Beach in Worthing!

Want to just play with friends? Prefer to avoid the pressure of tournament competition? Want to meet new people to play with? Like to hang out on the beach in a relaxed vibe with awesome tunes? Or just want to turn up and play without the commitment of pre-registering in advance? Either way, All You Can Play beach volleyball is just right for you!

AYCP is available on various weekends throughout the entire year at both of our venues. Simply check the events calendar or the homepage to find out exactly when!

In case you didn't already know, here's how it works:

LEYTON

Courts are available non-stop from 10:00am to 5:00pm (or 11:00am to 4:00pm between 1 October and 31 March).

  • Step 1: Turn up at any time during the day - alone or with friends. No need to pre-register!

  • Step 2: Pay £13 per person or just £8 if you are a registered member of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club. Please present your Club membership card (downloadable as a PDF from our registration system).

  • Step 3: Just play! All levels are welcome!


WORTHING

Courts are available in two separate sessions:

Session 1: 09:00am to 1:00pm (or 10:00am to 1:00pm between 1 October and 31 March).

Session 2: 1:00pm to 5:00pm (or 1:00pm to 4:00pm between 1 October and 31 March).

  • Step 1: Turn up at any time during one of the two sessions - alone or with friends.

  • Step 2: Pay £11 per person or just £6 if you are a registered member of SideOut Beach Volleyball Club. Please present your Club membership card (downloadable as a PDF from our registration system).

  • Step 3: Just play! All levels are welcome! Spaces will be limited to a maximum of 20 people at any one time in order to guarantee more court time for everyone.

    If you'd like to stay and continue playing after session 1, you are welcome to do so for an additional cost of £2 per person, but only if there is enough space, as priority will be given to people arriving for session 2.


See you on the sand!
