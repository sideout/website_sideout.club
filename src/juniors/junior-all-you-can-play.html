Junior All You Can Play



Everyone’s favourite day of beach volleyball is now available for Juniors at Leyton Beach!

Are you under 18 and wanting to improve your skills while getting some extra games with your friends? Want to meet new people to play with in a relaxed, fun, safe and welcoming environment? Either way, Junior All You Can Play beach volleyball is just right for you!

Junior AYCP is available to boys and girls of all levels and ages under 18. The events take place at Leyton Beach between 10:00am and 12:00 noon on each of our regular AYCP days. Simply check the events calendar or the homepage to find out exactly when these are happening!

Here’s how it works:

LEYTON

Courts are available non-stop from 10:00am to 5:00pm (or 11:00am to 4:00pm between 1 October and 31 March).

  • Step 1: Turn up at any time from 10:00am - alone or with friends. No need to pre-register!

  • Step 2: Pay £3per person.

  • Step 3: Just play! At least 2 courts will be available until 12:00 noon for Junior AYCP. !


See you on the sand!
