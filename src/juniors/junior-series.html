SideOut Junior Series



The Juniors' time to shine!

New for this year, the SideOut Junior Series is an opportunity for young boys and girls to compete and further develop their beach volleyball skills. All players born on or after 1 September 2003, regardless of ability or experience are not just welcome, but strongly encouraged to come and play!

We hope to see you all on the sand!

AGE GROUPS
  • U14s: Players must be born on or after 1 September 2006
  • U18s: Players must be born on or after 1 September 2002
UK BEACH TOUR
  • All tournaments are part of the UK Junior Beach Tour
  • All players must hold a valid Full UKBT Membership (in addition to a SideOut 365-day Membership or Single Event Pass)
FORMAT
  • Pool play followed by elimination rounds
  • Minimum 3 matches per team per tournament
ENTRY RULES & PROCESS
  • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
  • Registration closes when the tournament is full, or on the Friday before each tournament.
  • Entry is first-come, first-served. Once you have entered and paid, you are in the tournament!
  • All teams will receive an email with full details, schedule and timings by the Friday before each tournament.
