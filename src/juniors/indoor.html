OUR PROGRAMME

We introduced indoor training sessions following the huge growth of the SideOut Junior Beach Programme in the summer of 2021, in order to allow our younger members to continue to train, play, compete and develop their skills throughout the winter months.

Like our Junior Beach Programme, these sessions are managed and delivered by a highly experienced team of coaches and our goal is to combine fun with top quality training and playing opportunities in a friendly and safe environment where our young athletes can make new friends, improve their fitness, develop their skills and - should they eventually choose to - pursue opportunities to compete at local, national or even international level!

VOLLEYBALL vs BEACH VOLLEYBALL

Apart from the obvious difference – beach volleyball is played on sand, while indoor volleyball is played on a hard surface – there are also several other differences between the 2 incredibly popular Olympic sports, including court size, team sizes, scoring and tactics. However, they also have many similarities and we strongly believe that the skillsets required in each discipline are complementary to each other and that young athletes can benefit greatly from getting quality coaching in both volleyball and beach volleyball.

A good case in point is our partner volleyball club, Polonia SideOut London. The team is coached by our very own Mark Kontopoulos and they are not only the current National Champions in indoor volleyball, but they also feature 6 players who are ranked in the top 8 beach volleyball teams in the UK, including the 2020 and 2021 National Champions!

Junior Indoor Training, Leytonstone: Frequently Asked Questions


WHERE & WHEN WILL MY CHILD TRAIN?

All of our indoor sessions will take place in the sports hall at Pastures Youth Centre, 15 Davies Lane, Leytonstone, E11 3DR.

During Term 1 (5th October 2021 to 13th December 2021), sessions are every Tuesday afternoon and all players (ages 5-12) train from 5:00pm to 6:00pm each day.

During Term 2 (4th January 2022 to 31st March 2022), sessions are available every Tuesday and Thursday afternoon. All players aged 5-8 train on Tuesdays only, while players aged 9-12 can train on Tuesdays and/or Thursdays.

WHAT ARE THE AGE GROUPS AND LEVELS?

Our indoor sessions currently cater for boys and girls aged 5-12 only. We welcome kids of all levels - from complete beginners, to more experienced players who already train or compete regularly in either indoor or beach volleyball. Training groups are organised based on both age and ability, so everyone will always be training and playing in an appropriate group.

WHO ARE THE COACHES?

We have assembled a team of extremely passionate and highly skilled coaches with diverse and extensive playing and coaching experience at all levels of the sport. All of our coaches are fully qualified and DBS checked and our Club is affiliated with Volleyball England, volleyball and beach volleyball's national governing body.
