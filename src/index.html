2020 Season Update (28 July 2020)

Tournaments and open play are back!

We are very excited to be able to offer a full complement of tournaments, All-You-Can-Play, training sessions, bookings and open play at both SideOut Beach in Leyton and SideOut Beach in Worthing.

We of course continue to “Train Smart, Play Safe”, so all of our activities are in full compliance with Government and Volleyball England rules and guidance. You can find Volleyball England’s latest detailed guidance here.
