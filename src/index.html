Latest News (19 March 2021): We are re-opening very soon!

As things currently stand, we are expecting to resume all our Club activities at both Leyton and Worthing from Monday 29th March!

We've been working hard in the background to be ready to go as soon as we are allowed to start again and we have a packed schedule of training, play and tournaments planned for 2021. We are expecting updated guidance from Sport England and Volleyball England around the 22nd of March, so we hope to be able to confirm everything immediately after that. Watch this space!

You can also see Volleyball England’s regularly updated guidance here.

It's been a very long wait, but we are so excited to be seeing you all on the sand again very, very soon!
