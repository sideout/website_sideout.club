It's Our 10th Birthday!

SideOut turns 10 this August and we're celebrating at Leyton Beach with 3 action-packed days of beach volleyball and partying!

Whether you want to compete, play, watch, eat, drink, party - or all of the above - this is a weekend not to be missed!



SideOut Turns 10!

SideOut Family! We can't believe it has already been 10 years! We're so excited to be hitting such a great milestone together and we are so grateful for the love and support you've all shown us since day 1! To help us celebrate and say a small but heartfelt "thank you" to every single one of you, we would like to invite you to an action-packed long weekend of beach volleyball and partying on 28-30 August 2021 at Leyton Beach.

WHAT'S THE PLAN?

The action starts on Saturday 28th August with the London Grand Slam, a 5-star tournament on the UK Beach Tour which will feature the best of the best players in the UK.

On Sunday the 29th, the top 8 teams per gender will be competing for the London Grand Slam title in the quarters, semis and finals. And that's not all, because there's another tournament planned! The 10th Birthday Tournament will be a 3-star event on the UKBT and will be an opportunity for all our members to play in a more laid back but still competitive event with lots of cool prizes to be won.

On the Sunday night, it's PARTY TIME!

And on Monday the 30th, it's all about FREE All You Can Play and an on-the-spot "winner takes all" fun tournament!

WHAT ELSE?

There will also be a licensed bar and hot food available on site throughout the long weekend, as well as many freebies and other surprises!

Check out all the details below!
